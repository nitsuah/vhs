VHS Condition Guide

GreatPlays perfectly. Case tight, no cracks. Label clean. May show minor shelf wear only.
GoodPlays well with no tracking issues. Cosmetic wear: scuffs, faded label, small cracks acceptable.
FairPlays but may need tracking adjustment. Noticeable wear: cracked case, water damage, heavy scuffs.
PoorWon't play or major issues. Damaged tape, broken shell, severe mold. For parts / display only.
